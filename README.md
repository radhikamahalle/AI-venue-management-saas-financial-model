# AI-Powered Venue Management SaaS Financial Model

## Overview

This repository showcases a Financial Planning & Analysis (FP&A) model developed for an early-stage AI-powered Venue & Event Management SaaS startup.

The model was created to support budgeting, forecasting, fundraising, cash flow planning and long-term business strategy.

> **Note:** Company-specific information has been anonymized to maintain confidentiality.

---

# Business Overview

The business provides an AI-powered SaaS platform that helps sports venues, entertainment facilities and event organizers manage venue operations, bookings, scheduling, financial workflows, incident management and operational planning through a unified platform.

---

# Business Objective

The financial model was developed to:

- Forecast Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR)
- Plan subscription revenue
- Estimate operating expenses
- Build hiring plans
- Forecast cash flow
- Estimate funding requirements
- Support investor fundraising
